Source: Egyptian Pyramid Texts (summary seed)

These funerary utterances frame kingship, ascent, and divine continuity. Cosmic order is sustained through ritual language, naming, and alignment with celestial patterns. Death is not merely ending but transition into a reordered sacred status mediated by spoken formula and divine affiliation.

Comparative hooks:
- Speech and naming as transformative forces.
- Kingship intertwined with cosmic order.
- Afterlife transition as patterned ascent.
- Ritual text as active participation in sacred reality.
