Source: Enuma Elish (summary seed)

Cosmic beginnings unfold through generations of divine beings associated with primordial waters. Conflict among gods culminates in a champion deity establishing rule through victory over chaos. The world is shaped from the aftermath of divine struggle, and humanity is fashioned to bear labor associated with temple order and service.

Comparative hooks:
- Primeval waters as an origin motif.
- Creation linked to kingship and authority.
- Cosmos established through overcoming chaos.
- Human purpose tied to sacred order and ritual economy.
